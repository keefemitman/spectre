Detailed Description

template<size_t Dim>
struct ScalarWave::ComputeDuDt< Dim >

Compute the time derivative of the evolved variables of the first-order scalar wave system.

The evolution equations for the first-order scalar wave system are given by:

\begin{align} \partial_t\Psi = & -\Pi \\ \partial_t\Phi_i = & -\partial_i \Pi + \gamma_2 (\partial_i\Psi - \Phi_i) \\ \partial_t\Pi = & - \delta^{ij}\partial_i\Phi_j \end{align}

where \(\Psi\) is the scalar field, \(\Pi=-\partial_t\Psi\) is the conjugate momentum to \(\Psi\), \(\Phi_i=\partial_i\Psi\) is an auxiliary variable, and \(\gamma_2\) is the constraint damping parameter.
