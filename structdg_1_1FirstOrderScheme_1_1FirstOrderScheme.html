Detailed Description

template<size_t Dim, typename VariablesTag, typename NumericalFluxComputerTag, typename TemporalIdTag>
struct dg::FirstOrderScheme::FirstOrderScheme< Dim, VariablesTag, NumericalFluxComputerTag, TemporalIdTag >

Boundary contributions for a first-order DG scheme.

Computes Eq. (2.20) in [61] and lifts it to the volume (see dg::lift_flux) on all mortars that touch an element. The resulting boundary contributions are added to the DG operator data in db::add_tag_prefix<TemporalIdTag::template step_prefix, VariablesTag>.
